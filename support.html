Shipping Information

Our standard shipping times are 5-7 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. Please contact our support team to initiate a return. Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after receiving the returned item.

Payment Methods

We accept credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and UPI payments through secure payment gateways.

Order Status

You can check your order status by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.

Account Creation

To create an account, click on "My Account" at the top of the page and fill out the registration form.

Order Cancellation

If you need to cancel your order, please contact our support team within 24 hours of placing the order. After this period, cancellation may not be possible.

Product Information

Detailed product information, including specifications and user manuals, can be found on each product's page under the "Description" tab.

Technical Support

Yes, we offer technical support for all our products. You can reach out to our support team via email or phone for any technical assistance.

Bulk Orders

We accommodate bulk orders for educational institutions and businesses. Please contact our sales team for special pricing and arrangements.

Privacy and Data Protection

Your personal information is protected through our comprehensive privacy policy. We use secure servers and encryption to ensure your data remains confidential.
